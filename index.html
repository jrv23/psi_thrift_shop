Get involved

Volunteers make it possible for Project Safe to provide victims and survivors of domestic violence with more extensive services than our small staff couldn't provide alone.

Volunteer Opportunites include

Badge cataloger

Maintaining and organizing the name badges of volunteers along with cataloging any changes made to the current list of volunteers.

Value Assessor

Pricing rare or unusual items and suggesting the sale of specific items on Craigslist, eBay, or similar platforms. Some experience required.

Display Designer

Creating displays for storefront and creating promotional materials to attract customers along with making sure all visuals are up to date.
